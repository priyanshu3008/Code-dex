Abel Makkonen Tesfaye, known professionally as The Weeknd, is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and record producer renowned for his unique voice and innovative contributions to contemporary music. Born on February 16, 1990, in Toronto, Ontario, Tesfaye emerged from an enigmatic, underground presence to a globally acclaimed superstar.
