PRO FOOTBALL; Dayne's Future Decided, But Fassel's Not Telling Yet

Ron Dayne's immediate future with the Giants has been decided. He just does not know what it is yet.
Giants Coach Jim Fassel said yesterday that he had made all the decisions concerning his final cut to a 53-man roster, but he would not tell the players until today. And the team does not intend to make the cuts public until 4 p.m. tomorrow, the N.F.L deadline.
This tortured process holds the fate of many players in suspended animation, but the drama surrounding Dayne, the Giants' No. 1 draft pick in 2000, is the thickest.
